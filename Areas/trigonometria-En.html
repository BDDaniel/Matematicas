Trigonometry


Trigonometry is a branch of mathematics whose etymological meaning is 'the measurement of triangles'. In general terms, trigonometry is the study of the trigonometric ratios: sine, cosine, tangent, cotangent, secant and cosecant.

Topics

Rectangular triangles

A right triangle is any triangle that has a right angle, that is, an angle of 90 degrees.

Trigonometric functions

Trigonometric functions are functions determined with the objective of extending the definition of trigonometric ratios to all real and complex numbers.
