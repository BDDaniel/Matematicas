Statistics


Statistics is the branch of mathematics that studies the variability, collection, organization, analysis, interpretation, and presentation of data, as well as the random process that generates them following the laws of probability.

Topics

Measures of central tendency

Measures of central tendency are statistical measures that attempt to summarize a set of values into a single value.

Arithmetic mean, mode and median

The arithmetic mean, also called average or simply mean, is obtained as the sum of a set of values divided by the total number of summands.

Measures of dispersion

In statistics, measures of dispersion is the degree to which a distribution is stretched or compressed.

Variance and standard deviation

The variance of a random variable is a measure of dispersion defined as the expectation of the square of the deviation of that variable from its mean.

Probability

Probability is the likelihood that a phenomenon or event will occur, given certain circumstances. It is expressed as a percentage.
