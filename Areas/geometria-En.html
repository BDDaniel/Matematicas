Geometry


Geometry is a branch of mathematics that deals with the study of the properties of figures in the plane or space, including: points, lines, planes, polytopes (such as parallels, perpendiculars, curves, surfaces, polygons, polyhedra, etc.).
Geometry is one of the oldest sciences. Initially, it constituted a body of practical knowledge concerning lengths, areas and volumes.
Its origins go back to the solution of concrete measurement problems. It has its practical application in applied physics, mechanics, architecture, geography, cartography, astronomy, nautics, topography, ballistics, etc., and is useful in the preparation of designs and even in the manufacture of handicrafts.
